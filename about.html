IT SKILLS OLYMPICS

The IT Skills Olympics is an annual event dedicated to empowering students and professionals through technology-based competitions. Organized by the University of Makati, the event brings together participants to test their skills in fields like web design, cybersecurity, programming, and more.

Our Goals

13th IT Skills olympics goals with the theme: Augmenting security towards the techno-utopia

Register now

1 Strengthen Collaboration

Encourage teamwork to develop secure and futuristic technologies in pursuit of a techno-utopia.

2 Drive Innovation Towards Utopia

Inspire participants to develop solutions that integrate security and technological advancement for societal benefit.

3 Enhance Cybersecurity Skills

Equip participants with the knowledge to build secure systems and counteract modern digital threats.
